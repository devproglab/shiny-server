Trade diversification database - a tool at early stages of development which aims to provide access to a set of measures for trade diversification. Currently the Theil index (alongside with its "between" and "within" decomposition) is provided, which allows to examine export basket product diversification along the intensive and extensive margins. Future releases will feature the HHI index for product diversification, as well as various measures of market diversification. Bulk download options are scheduled to be available in summer 2021.
